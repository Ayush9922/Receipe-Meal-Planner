Paneer Curry

Ingredients:

  • 250g Paneer
  • 1 cup Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup Onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Capsicum, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds.
  2. Step 2: Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Step 3: Add tomatoes, capsicum, and paneer. Cook for 5-7 minutes.

Spicy Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp Garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Let it sit for 1 hour.
  2. Step 2: Cook the marinated chicken in a pan until it's fully cooked.
  3. Step 3: Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots, beans), boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup Tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup Capsicum, finely chopped
  • 4 tbsp Pav Bhaji masala
  • 1/2 cup Butter

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: In a pan, sauté onions until golden brown. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  2. Step 2: Add boiled and mashed vegetables, pav bhaji masala, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Step 3: Cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add butter and mix.
